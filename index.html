We have a pack of cards at our disposal. We will only use those from 1 to 6. We mix them, and arrange them in a circle. We choose a card, which will be the first, as well as a direction in which we will turn. The player mentally chooses a card among the first 6. From this card, he advances in the chosen direction of the number marked on the card. The ace has a score of 1. He arrives at a second card, repeats the process and so on until returning among the first 6 cards. The magician must guess the arrival card.
